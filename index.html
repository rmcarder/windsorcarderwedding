Travel and Accommodations

We have blocks at two hotels, listed below. Please use the links provided here to book to get the block rates. There are also plentiful AirBnBs and VRBOs in the area.

School buses will provide transportation to and from the wedding venue on Saturday. They will leave from the Crowne Plaza hotel at 3pm. There will be return trips at 9pm and 10:45pm. More Details

How are you handling COVID-19?

We recognize the impacts and uncertainty of the ongoing pandemic. We will continue to monitor the situation and adjust as needed. We strongly encourage COVID-19 vaccination and testing before you join any of our gatherings. We are expecting approximately 125 people, so we understand if gatherings don't feel comfortable for you at this time. We encourage wearing of masks in indoor settings, such as the bus ride to the venue, and please feel free to wear masks at any time or social distance to your liking. The Saturday wedding celebration will take place outdoors at Lady Luck Gardens. The Friday welcome drinks will take place partially indoors and outdoors at Cellarest Beer Project.

What will the Saturday wedding event entail?

We will have transportation to and from the venue, a ceremony, a cocktail hour that flows into the dinner reception, a dance party, and a fire pit. Beverages (alcoholic and non-alcoholic) and food will be provided by Jack's Mobile Bar and our caterer, King of Fire. We will have appetizers, wood-fired pizzas, and salad for dinner, and desserts. We will be asking about your dietary restrictions with the RSVP. There will not be a formal seated dinner, rather plenty of spaces and tables for guests to eat and drink throughout the night as you'd like.

What should I wear?

For the Saturday wedding celebration at Lady Luck, we encourage you to wear what makes you feel happiest and most comfortable. We hope the gathering will feel like a festive garden party. The event will be subject to the natural elements! The venue has a permanent covered event pavilion and paved floor, and we have rented some tents in case of rain. We will be outside, so be mindful that there could possibly be mud or unstable, grassy ground. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions.

Will I be able to use Uber and Lyft to get around?

Ubers and Lyfts are plentiful in downtown and West Asheville. However, they will not be available to get home from the wedding venue. There is neither cell phone service nor a supply of rideshare vehicles there. Therefore we highly recommend that you plan tpo take the school buses to and from the venue, which leave at 3pm from the Crowne Plaza, and will have return trips at 9pm and 10:45pm. For the Friday night welcome drinks, Uber and Lyft should be easy.

What about mobility?

We have planned for the ceremony, cocktail hour, and dinner reception to take place across different areas of the Lady Luck Venue (approximately 2-5 minute walk between areas). Our amazing planning team at Rising Fern Events will ensure everyone knows where to be at each point. If you will need assistance moving from place to place, please let us know so we can help coordinate a car. There will be two, all-gender bathrooms. The right bathroom is wheelchair accessible.

Some important notes about safety and the venue:

Be careful near the pond. You are responsible for your safety. No children alone by the pond please. All children must be supervised the entire length of your contracted stay. Please keep kids out of the farm and flower garden beds.

Lady Luck Gardens asks that all guests refrain from traveling through any closed farm gate, or over to their private residence, including the yard, the creek by their house, the little pond by their house and the workshop barn. They ask that guests be respectful of the farm crops and land by staying on designated paths. They also ask that guests do not try to pet any of the farm animals or bother them in any way.
