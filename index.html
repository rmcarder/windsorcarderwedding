Travel and Accommodations

We so appreciate the time and resources it will take to come to Asheville.

If you're flying in, Asheville Regional Airport (AVL) would be the most convenient, as it's only a 20 minute drive/Uber to downtown. It's a small airport, however, and may not have too many direct flights. Another option is to fly to a larger airport and rent a car, such as Charlotte (CLT - about a 2 hour drive), or Atlanta (ATL - about a 3.5 hour drive).

We have blocks at two hotels, listed below. Please use the links provided here to book to get the block rates. We tried to secure the lowest price possible for the group rates, but given the busy weekend these still ended up higher than we would have liked. At a potentially lower price point, there are also plentiful AirBnBs, VRBOs, and other hotel options in the area. We recommend booking early if you can, as it's a very popular weekend in the area.

School buses will provide transportation to and from the wedding venue on Saturday. They will leave from the Crowne Plaza hotel at 3pm (there is plenty of parking at the Crowne Plaza, and it is accessible via Uber/Lyft). It will be about a 35 minute ride. There will be return trips at 9pm and 10:45pm. We highly recommend that you take the provided transportation, even if you are not staying at the Crowne Plaza. The roads near the venue are dark and winding, so we do not recommend driving, and Ubers/Lyfts will not be available from the venue.

Crowne Plaza

The Crowne Plaza is across the river from downtown and close to West Asheville. It has a large property with ziplining, mountain bike trails, and a par 3 golf course. Buses to the wedding celebration will leave from here. You can find reviews here. The room block is $245 per night, and is available Thursday-Monday, or any combination of days within that period. Use group code WCA. Rooms must be booked by August 31 to secure this rate. Pet friendly by request.

More Details

How are you handling COVID-19?

We recognize the impacts and uncertainty of the ongoing pandemic. We will continue to monitor the situation and share recommended practices closer to the event. We are expecting up to 125 people, so we understand if gatherings don't feel comfortable for you at this time. The Saturday wedding celebration will take place outdoors at Lady Luck Gardens. The Friday welcome party will take place partially indoors and outdoors at Cellarest Beer Project.

What will the Saturday wedding event entail?

We will have transportation to and from the venue, a ceremony, a cocktail hour that flows into the dinner reception, a dance party, and a fire pit. Beverages (alcoholic and non-alcoholic) and food will be provided by Jack's Mobile Bar and our caterer, King of Fire. We will have appetizers, wood-fired pizzas, salads, and desserts. We will be asking about your dietary restrictions with the RSVP. There will not be a formal seated dinner, rather plenty of spaces and tables for guests to eat and drink throughout the night.

What should I wear?

For the Saturday wedding celebration at Lady Luck, we encourage you to wear what makes you happiest and most comfortable. We hope the gathering will feel like a festive garden party. The event will be subject to the natural elements! The venue has a permanent covered event pavilion and paved floor, and we have rented some tents in case of rain. We will be outside, so be mindful that there could possibly be mud or unstable, grassy ground. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions.

Will I be able to use Uber and Lyft to get around?

Ubers and Lyfts are plentiful in downtown and West Asheville. However, they will not be available to get home from the wedding venue. There is neither reliable cell phone service nor a supply of rideshare vehicles there. Therefore we highly recommend that you plan to take the school buses to and from the venue, which leave at 3pm from the Crowne Plaza, and will have return trips at 9pm and 10:45pm. For the Friday night welcome party, Uber and Lyft should be easy.

What about mobility?

We have planned for the ceremony, cocktail hour, and dinner reception to take place across different areas of the Lady Luck venue (approximately 2-5 minute walk between areas). Our amazing planning team at Rising Fern Events will ensure everyone knows where to be at each point. If you will need assistance moving from place to place, please let us know so we can help coordinate a car. There will be two restrooms, and the right-side one is wheelchair accessible.
